Who Are We?

Alpha Learning is a premier provider of exam preparation services for finance and accounting designations, including CFA, FRM, CPA, and CSC and ongoing tutoring services for business related subjects for BCom and MBA students. Our goal is to help students overcome all learning hurdles and succeed in their exams by improving their problem solving and exam writing skills.

The courses are designed to maximize learning in a collaborative, supportive environment.
